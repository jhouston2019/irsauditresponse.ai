CP2000 Notice (Most Common)

The CP2000 is the most common IRS notice, typically sent when there's a discrepancy between what you reported on your tax return and what the IRS has on file from third parties (like W-2s, 1099s, etc.).

What it looks like:

"We found a difference between the amount of income you reported on your tax return and the amount we received from third parties..."
