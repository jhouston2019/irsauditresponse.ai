Received an IRS Audit Letter You Don't Understand?

What This Means

An IRS audit letter means the IRS is reviewing part or all of your tax return and is asking for clarification or documentation. Most audits are conducted by mail and focus on specific items, not your entire return.

Audits are common and do not automatically mean the IRS believes you committed fraud or intentional wrongdoing.

What Happens If You Ignore It

  • The IRS may make decisions without your input
  • Deductions or income positions may be disallowed
  • Additional tax, penalties, or interest may be assessed
